Lavender Town Syndrome

In the early 2010s, an urban legend claimed that hundreds of Japanese children had committed suicide in the 1990s as a result of the music in the game, speculating that high pitched tones and binaural beats caused headaches and erratic behavior that led to their deaths. But what if was a experiment by the Pokemon Company. An experiment the went horrily wrong.
